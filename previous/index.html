Our Roots

The Bellamy Family had its formation through the union of Jack and Nancy Bellamy in Carnesville, Ga.  They later moved to Toccoa, GA where they reared a family of ten children.  The Bellamy reunion includes the descendants of Jack and Nancy's children.  This reunion also includes the descendants of a cousin, Tillie Bellamy Brawner.
